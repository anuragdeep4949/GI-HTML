Hypothetico-Deductive Reasoning

What do you mean by Hypothesis?
A hypothesis is a possible explanation for an observation or problems that can further be tested by experimentation.
What do you mean by Prediction?
A prediction is a guess what might happen based on observation.
What do you mean by Observation?
Observation is the active acquisition of information from a primary source.
What do you mean by conclusion?
Conclusion is a summary of the experiment's results, and how those results match up to your hypothesis.
